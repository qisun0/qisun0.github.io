I am currently a research scientist at Adobe Research and an incoming assistant professor at New York University. Previously, I obtained my PhD at Stony Brook University. My research interests lie in perceptual computer graphics, human-computer interaction, VR/AR, and visual optics. I am a recipient of the IEEE Virtual Reality Best Dissertation Award.


Prospective students. I am taking new PhD students @ NYU Tandon. Shoot me an email if you are interested in conducting and publishing top research for future VR/AR and perceptual computer graphics, including real-time rendering, vision science, display/imaging optics, and multimodal perception.
