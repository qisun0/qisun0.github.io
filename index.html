Qi Sun is an assistant professor at New York University. Before joining NYU, he was a research scientist at Adobe Research. He received his PhD at Stony Brook University. His research interests lie in perceptual computer graphics, VR/AR, visual optics, human-computer interaction. He is a recipient of the IEEE Virtual Reality Best Dissertation Award.

For details, please visit his group website: www.immersivecomputinglab.org


Prospective students. I am taking new PhD students @ NYU Tandon for 2022 Fall. We conduct and publish top research for future VR/AR and computer graphics, including real-time rendering, vision science, display/imaging optics, and multimodal perception. Contact me with your resume especially if you have background in physics/EE/hardware.
